What is Bullying?

Bullying is unwanted, aggressive behaviour that commonly consists of teasing, name calling, hitting, fighting, swearing, and being hurt. Bullying can be classified into 4 categories; Cyber Bullying, Physical Bullying, Verbal Bullying, and Relational Bullying.

Cyber Bullying involves any form of continued harassment, racism, sexism, or homophobic behaviour.

Physical Bullying involves anything to do with being physically hurt, actions such as pushing, punching, slapping, hitting, biting, and scratching.

Verbal Bullying involves behaviours such as yelling, name calling, harassment, and teasing.
